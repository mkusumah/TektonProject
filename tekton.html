Welcome to Tekton

About Us

Tekton makes high-quality tools for professionals and DIY builders. We’re based in Michigan and operate as a proud independent tool company.

Our Mission

We aim to empower builders through reliable, affordable tools that support creativity, safety, and productivity.

We believe tools should be built to last — with thoughtful design and straightforward function.

Customer satisfaction and tool innovation are our top priorities.

Our Story & History

Tekton was founded in 1991 in Grand Rapids, Michigan. What started as a small family venture has grown into a respected national brand.

The word “Tekton” is from the ancient Greek language. It is similar to the word “technician” and shares its origin with words like “technical” and “technique.” A Tekton is a person of skill, particularly with the hands.

We began with a handful of products — mostly wrench sets — but quickly expanded based on customer needs. Today, we continue to design, test, and ship tools from our Michigan location.
